About Us

Who We Are

Broad Experiential Ltd is an Out Of Home marketing company focused on consumer-facing branding solutions across East Africa.

Over the years we have acquired vast experience in Experiential Marketing, Digital Marketing, Printing, Branding and Design across the East African region. We have forged relationships that enable us offer affordable and competitive rates.

We take the headache off you through:

  • ◆ Negotiations – all rates & county fees associated with branding activities.
  • ◆ Relationships – we foster strong relationships across the country. There is nowhere we can not ensure your branding appears!
  • ◆ Reporting – we will ensure that reports and updates are on your desk on time.

Leading branding solution provider for enterprise & SME in East Africa.
